mission "Sol: Urgent Councilor 1"
	landing
	name "Direct Course to Sol"
	description "Carry Councilor Ilya Taranenko directly to <planet>. The deadline is <date>."
	source
		system "Alpha Centauri"
		system "Altair"
		system "Caph"
		system "Denebola"
		system "Sirius"
		system "Vega"
	destination "Earth"
	passengers 1
	deadline 3
	blocked "You need a free bunk to take Ilya Taranenko aboard."
	to offer
		random 50
		not "sol councilor urgent: done"
	on offer
		conversation
			`In the spaceport lounge, a man in a rumpled suit stares at his datapad. When he notices you, he straightens.`
			`"Captain?" He hesitates, and you can hear the fatigue in his voice. "I'm Ilya Taranenko, a councilor from the Inner Worlds. My transport to Sol has been delayed, and I have to be there in person for a floor vote in <date> days. Detours are a political luxury I can't afford."`
			`He sizes up your ship as if already doing the math. "I'll pay in cash. I just need a bunk and your discretion. Take me straight to Earth, as fast as you can."`
			choice
				`"Get aboard. We'll depart immediately."`
					accept
				`"That's too tight. Find someone else."`
	on complete
		payment 50000
		"reputation: Federation" = 1
		set "sol councilor urgent: done"
		dialog `Taranenko exhales as you set down in Earth's spaceport. "Just in time for the vote," he says, already moving toward the exit. He presses the agreed payment into your hand. "You were reliable today. I will need a quick ride to the Dirt Belt to meet with some locals."`
